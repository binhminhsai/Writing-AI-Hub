### Câu điều kiện (Conditional sentences)
1. **First Conditional**
   - ""If technological advancements continue at this pace, pollution levels could significantly decrease.""
2. **Second Conditional**
   - ""If every individual were to change their consumption habits, the impact on pollution could be profound.""
3. **Third Conditional**
   - ""If governments had invested in green technologies decades ago, the current level of pollution might have been lower.""

### Câu bị động (Passive voice)
1. ""Solutions to pollution are often provided by technological innovations.""
2. ""New policies are being implemented to encourage sustainable living.""
3. ""Environmental data is collected to track pollution sources.""

### Mệnh đề quan hệ (Relative clauses)
1. ""Technologies that reduce emissions are becoming increasingly efficient.""
2. ""Individuals who engage in sustainable practices contribute to a cleaner environment.""
3. ""The policies, which were introduced recently, have started to show results.""

### Câu ghép (Compound sentences)
1. ""Technological solutions are effective, but they need to be complemented by behavioral changes.""
2. ""The government has introduced stricter regulations, and industries are beginning to comply.""
3. ""Pollution affects air quality, and it also harms water and soil.""

### Mệnh đề nhượng bộ (Clauses of concession)
1. ""Although technology can mitigate pollution, human behavior must also change.""
2. ""Even though there are advancements, pollution remains a significant issue.""
3. ""While some believe technology is enough, others argue for a behavioral shift.""

### Chủ ngữ giả (Dummy subject)
1. ""It is essential to combine both technology and behavioral changes to tackle pollution.""
2. ""There are many challenges in implementing sustainable practices globally.""
3. ""It seems that awareness campaigns can significantly impact behavior.""

### Đảo ngữ (Inversion)
1. ""Not only is technological advancement necessary, but so is a change in human behavior.""
2. ""Seldom have we seen such a coordinated global effort to combat pollution.""
3. ""Rarely does technology alone solve environmental issues.""

### Mệnh đề danh từ (Noun clauses)
1. ""What is crucial is a holistic approach to solving pollution.""
2. ""The fact that human activity affects pollution is undeniable.""
3. ""How individuals respond to environmental policies will determine their success.""

Using these structures will enhance the complexity and clarity of your writing, which can potentially improve your IELTS Writing score."
